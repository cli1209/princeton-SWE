Join SWE!

If you are a Princeton student and would like to join SWE follow these three easy steps:

1. Email swe@princeton.edu and request to be added to the email list.
2. Go to www.swe.org and register with National SWE. National membership costs $20 a year. As a national member you will receive the quarterly SWE magazine, have access to web-based seminars, invites to SWE sponsored conferences, as well as be linked into a powerful network of women engineers.
3. Come to a SWE event! Every month SWE holds at least one study break. We often host job recruiters and have plans in the works for a luncheon speaker series. We also need your help in running events geared towards the community, so VOLUNTEER to help out!

Have a SWEet day and we hope that you will join us in making Princeton a great place to be a woman engineer!
